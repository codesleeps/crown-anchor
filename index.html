Game Instructions & Rules

🎯 How to Play

Crown & Anchor is a classic dice game where you bet on symbols and win when they appear on the dice.

  1. Select a chip value ($1, $5, $10, or $25)
  2. Click on betting squares to place bets
  3. Roll the dice to see the results
  4. Win money when your symbols appear!

💰 Winning Examples

  • Bet $10 on Crown: If 2 dice show Crown, win $20
  • Bet $5 on Heart: If 1 die shows Heart, win $5
  • Triple Match: If all 3 dice show your symbol, win 3x your bet!

🚀 Quick Betting

  • All Red: Bets on both Heart and Diamond
  • All Black: Bets on both Club and Spade
  • Royals: Bets on both Crown and Anchor
  • Bet Max: Places maximum bet on all squares
