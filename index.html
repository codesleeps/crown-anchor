üéØ How to Play

Crown & Anchor is a classic dice game where you bet on symbols and win when they appear on the dice.

  1. Select a chip value ($1, $5, $10, or $25)
  2. Click on betting squares to place bets
  3. Roll the dice to see the results
  4. Win money when your symbols appear!
